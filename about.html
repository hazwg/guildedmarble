Our story

Marble with meaning

We believe beautiful materials shouldn’t come at the planet’s expense. From quarry to project handover, we build sustainability and ethics into every step of our supply chain.

Ethically sourced at origin

We partner with family-owned quarries in Pakistan that uphold responsible working practices and invest in their local communities.

  • No child labour
  • Safe, regulated working conditions
  • Fair wages for local workers

By sourcing directly and transparently, we help support long-term economic growth in rural regions while ensuring the integrity of every block we supply.

Low-waste extraction & processing

Our quarry partners invest in equipment and processes that significantly reduce waste, energy use and water consumption.

  • Precision cutting to minimise offcuts
  • Water recycling systems to reduce freshwater usage
  • Slurry filtration aimed at low or zero discharge

These measures help protect local ecosystems and reduce the embodied impact of the stone that ultimately reaches your project.

Natural, durable and circular

Marble is a fully natural material with a long service life, which makes it a strong fit for circular design and responsible specification.

  • No synthetic additives or harmful binders
  • Long lifespans with minimal maintenance
  • Potential for reuse or repurposing in future projects

Conscious packaging & logistics

We’re continuously refining the way our stone is packed and shipped.

  • Recycled or responsibly sourced wooden crates
  • Biodegradable or minimal protective materials
  • Ocean-efficient shipping routes wherever possible

Supporting sustainable design teams

We collaborate with architects, designers and developers who prioritise environmental performance as much as aesthetics.

  • Support with sustainability documentation
  • Traceability and origin information on request
  • Guidance on selecting durable, low-impact stone for your specific application

If you’re working towards green building certifications, we’ll help you choose stone that supports your wider sustainability goals.
