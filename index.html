Rooted in Nature. Driven by Purpose.

Gilded Marble bridges the rich stone heritage of Pakistan with the precision and standards of the UK construction and design industry.

We partner with trusted family-run quarries committed to ethical labour, low-waste extraction and environmental responsibility. From concept to installation, we support architects, designers, builders and homeowners with stone that looks exceptional and stands for something.

Our role is simple: to be your long-term partner in creating timeless, sustainable spaces.

Learn more about our sustainability

Ethically sourced

We work directly with quarry partners who share our commitment to safe working conditions, fair wages and zero tolerance for child labour.

Low-waste extraction

Modern cutting, water recycling and slurry filtration technology help reduce waste, energy use and water consumption at the source.

Timeless & durable

Real stone offers natural variation, minimal maintenance and decades-long lifespans—supporting circular, conscious design.
